# Artist Spotlight — Shinji Kanda (神田真司)

**Tag:** MISC #3 · ARTIST SPOTLIGHT
**Subject:** Shinji Kanda — Japan's most distinct modern Pokémon TCG illustrator
**Slides:** 7 (hook + 5 body + CTA)
**Voice:** Clean, premium, educational. No hype.
**Source:** tcgame.com.au Artist Spotlight article + pokemon-card.com for card verification

## Why Kanda
- Debut: Magmar AR, VSTAR Universe (s12a #186/172), Dec 2, 2022
- Distinct style: ukiyo-e / kamon patterns / woodblock textures → looks like "art prints"
- Career explosion in ~3 years — already compared to Arita / Yuka Morii tier
- Strong visual identity: recognizable at a glance

- **Slide 04:** Magikarp AR (Pokemon 151 / SV2a) · Gyarados ex (Scarlet ex / SV1S) · Lugia · Entei
