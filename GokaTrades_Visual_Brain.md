# GokaTrades — Visual Brain

\---

## Identity

**Brand Name:** GokaTrades

**What We Do:** GokaTrades is an Indonesia-based Pokémon TCG business spanning educational social media content, a digital pack-opening app, and US-to-Indonesia product resale.

**Brand Feel:** Clean · Premium · Playful

**Brand Boundaries:**

* Playful but never deceiving — we're a Pokémon card company rooted in fun, but honesty and transparency come first.

\---

## Audience

**Who They Are:** Pokémon TCG enthusiasts of all ages across Indonesia — from kids to adults, as long as they're passionate about collecting cards. They love the hobby but struggle to access it fairly.

**Main Frustration:** No reliable source for fairly priced sealed Pokémon products. The market is flooded with scalpers and scammers, and there's no single trusted hub for Pokémon TCG information.

**What They Actually Want:** Cheap prices and reliable Pokémon TCG information — all in one place, from a brand they can trust.

**How They Talk About It:**

* "Too many scalpers"
* "I don't know who to trust"
* "Where do I even find this stuff at a fair price?"

**Visual Preferences:** Bold, image-heavy content. Dark blurred backgrounds, big white block text, Pokémon card imagery front and centre, price movement indicators (red/green arrows), and swipeable carousel formats.

\---

## Voice Rules

### Always

* Short, punchy sentences
* Clean and direct — get to the point
* Educational tone — inform, don't hype
* Standard CTAs: "Follow for more," "Link in bio," "Drop a comment," or end with a question

### Never

* Profanity
* The word "scam" (stay positive, not defensive)
* Overhyped language: "OMG," "you NEED this," emoji spam, or crypto-bro energy ("moon," "cop," "send it")

### Voice Examples

* ✅ Sounds like GokaTrades: "Ascended Heroes dropped 15% — here's what to buy now."
* ❌ Doesn't sound like GokaTrades: "OMG guys you NEED to cop these cards before they moon!! 🚀🚀🚀"

## Colour System

### Palette

* **Primary Dark Background:** #111111 — Default slide background. Used on most slides.
* **Primary Light Background:** #F7F7F7 — Used inside white text banner blocks on hook slides.
* **Headline Text (on dark):** #FFFFFF — All headlines, titles, and primary text on dark backgrounds.
* **Headline Text (on light):** #111111 — All headlines and primary text on white banner blocks.
* **CTA / Highlight:** #FF4444 — Buttons, key words, emphasis text, price drops, and accent elements. Use sparingly.
* **Secondary Text:** #A0A0A0 — Captions, sources, supporting details, and muted info.
* **Alternate Light Background:** #E8E8E8 — Subtle variety when a lighter frame is needed.

### Usage Rules

* Carousel backgrounds stay dark (#111111) by default.
* Hook slides use a two-zone layout: blurred card art background on top, solid white (#F7F7F7) banner block on bottom.
* Red (#FF4444) is reserved for CTAs, key words, and emphasis only — never overused.
* Text on card images uses either a blur backdrop or solid #111111 overlay at \~70–80% opacity.
* Price movement arrows: red for drops, green for gains.

## Imagery Direction

### Aesthetic

Clean, modern, minimalist. Content is built around Pokémon card imagery on dark backgrounds with bold white and red typography. No photos of people — the cards are the hero. AI-generated images used for backgrounds, textures, and supporting visuals.

### Colour Treatment

Black, white, and red colour tone. High contrast. Everything sharp and premium.

### By Slide Position

* **Hook slides:** Card showcase (2–3 cards fanned/overlapping) with blurred card art background, price arrows, white text banner block at bottom.
* **Body slides:** Clean info layouts with card imagery, minimal clutter, text-focused.
* **CTA slides:** Red accent elements, strong contrast, clear action with branding.

### Off-Brand — Never Use

* Bright, colourful, busy backgrounds
* Corporate stock photos
* Cartoonish or childish illustrations
* Low-contrast or washed-out imagery
* Neon colours

* `.slide-inner` flex column, padding 80px 70px, centered

### Density

* Dense in the card/image zone — the card is the hero
* Clean and bold in the text zone — no clutter
* Max elements per body slide: 1 hero image + section header + 4–5 label/value rows + 1 footnote

## Content Architecture

### Carousel Length

5–8 slides, varies by topic.

### Type 1: Market Updates (Price Movements)

* **Slide 1 (Hook):** Set name + "Prices X days/months later" — card showcase with red/green arrows, white text block. 8 words max.
* **Slides 2–6 (Value):** Individual card price breakdowns — card image, price data, movement direction.
* **Final Slide (CTA):** Follow for more / swipe back / link in bio.

### Type 2: News / Information

* **Slide 1 (Hook):** Big headline, key visual, grabs attention. 8 words max.
* **Slides 2–5 (Value):** Breakdown of the news — context, details, what it means.
* **Slide 6+ (if big news):** Deeper analysis, community impact.
* **Final Slide (CTA):** Follow for more / drop a comment / link in bio.

### Rules Across Both

* Hook slide: 8 words max.
* Body slides: 15 words max.
* CTA slide should feel designed, not empty — include branding and visual treatment.
* Every slide has card imagery.
